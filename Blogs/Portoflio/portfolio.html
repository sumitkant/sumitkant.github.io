Branding for a nascent-stage EV renting startup

  • May 23, 2020
  • BrandingDesign

I took this project to explore something different before I start my journey at American Express as a data analyst. It wasn't chill work (though it was during when the december chill was setting in) but the outcome turned out to be more than satisfactory. I ended up learning about the startup qQuick, worked with amazing folks and delivered something that I enjoyed being part of.

About qQuick

qQuick (spelled quick) is an electric scooter renting start-up based in Delhi led by Aishwarya Mani Kachhal. This company aims at providing afforadable rentals for electric scooters for commuting short distance for as low as 1 Rupee per minute. Aishwarya reached out to me with to provide him with necessary branding and marketing material for launching this in Delhi.

Business Cards

The qQuick logo that was given was thin, so I chose a font that was similar in weight to the logo. The blood red color came along with the logo, hence the red colored business card. Also, the orientation is vertical, this was something that was not well thought, which in my mind was something non-traditional.
