# Bubblegum Pop

A bright and energetic theme built from pink and teal tones that conveys optimism, motion, and approachability.

## Color Palette

- **Background / White**: `#FFFFFF` - Chosen page and canvas background
- **Body Text / Dark Teal**: `#069494` - Primary readable text color
- **Primary Accent / Light Pink**: `#FF69B4` - Buttons, links, and key visual emphasis
- **Secondary Accent / Bright Teal**: `#00F0FF` - Highlights, badges, charts, and supporting details

## Typography

- **Headers**: Poppins Bold
- **Body Text**: Figtree

## Best Used For

Consumer apps, children's products, edtech, travel promotions, social campaigns, lively dashboards.
