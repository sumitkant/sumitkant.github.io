# Harbor Haze

A balanced and refined theme built from cyan and orange tones that conveys restraint, trust, and quiet sophistication.

## Color Palette

- **Background / Muted Orange**: `#AD9E90` - Chosen page and canvas background
- **Body Text / Muted Gold**: `#736F60` - Primary readable text color
- **Primary Accent / Soft Cyan**: `#5C8DC5` - Buttons, links, and key visual emphasis
- **Secondary Accent / Muted Cyan**: `#909EAE` - Highlights, badges, charts, and supporting details

## Typography

- **Headers**: Inter Display Bold
- **Body Text**: Public Sans

## Best Used For

Corporate presentations, editorial layouts, premium retail, consulting reports, minimalist product interfaces.
