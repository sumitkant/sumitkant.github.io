# Under the Moonlight

A airy and tranquil theme built from blue tones that conveys ease, focus, and gentle momentum.

## Color Palette

- **Background / Pale Blue**: `#CCCCFF` - Chosen page and canvas background
- **Body Text / Dark Blue**: `#292966` - Primary readable text color
- **Primary Accent / Light Blue**: `#A3A3CC` - Buttons, links, and key visual emphasis
- **Secondary Accent / Muted Blue**: `#5C5C99` - Highlights, badges, charts, and supporting details

## Typography

- **Headers**: Lexend Bold
- **Body Text**: Inter

## Best Used For

Wellness apps, travel guides, healthcare content, productivity tools, clean editorial experiences.
