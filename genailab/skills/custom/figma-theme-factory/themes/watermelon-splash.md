# Watermelon Splash

A bright and energetic theme built from pink, lime, and charcoal tones that conveys optimism, motion, and approachability.

## Color Palette

- **Background / Light Green**: `#ADEBB3` - Chosen page and canvas background
- **Body Text / Charcoal**: `#4A4A4A` - Primary readable text color
- **Primary Accent / Light Pink**: `#FC6C85` - Buttons, links, and key visual emphasis
- **Secondary Accent / Bright Lime**: `#89F336` - Highlights, badges, charts, and supporting details

## Typography

- **Headers**: Poppins Bold
- **Body Text**: Figtree

## Best Used For

Consumer apps, children's products, edtech, travel promotions, social campaigns, lively dashboards.
