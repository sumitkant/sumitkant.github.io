# Quiet Luxury

A balanced and refined theme built from orange and orange-red tones that conveys restraint, trust, and quiet sophistication.

## Color Palette

- **Background / Pale Orange**: `#F7E6CA` - Chosen page and canvas background
- **Body Text / Muted Coral**: `#AD9C8E` - Primary readable text color
- **Primary Accent / Light Orange**: `#E8D59E` - Buttons, links, and key visual emphasis
- **Secondary Accent / Light Coral**: `#D9BBB0` - Highlights, badges, charts, and supporting details

## Typography

- **Headers**: Plus Jakarta Sans Bold
- **Body Text**: Inter

## Best Used For

Corporate presentations, editorial layouts, premium retail, consulting reports, minimalist product interfaces.
