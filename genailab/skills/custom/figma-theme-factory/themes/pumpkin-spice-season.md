# Pumpkin Spice Season

A organic and restorative theme built from orange-red and gold tones that conveys freshness, connection, and grounded energy.

## Color Palette

- **Background / Deep Coral**: `#332216` - Chosen page and canvas background
- **Body Text / Dark Coral**: `#BE5103` - Primary readable text color
- **Primary Accent / Dark Coral**: `#8C4C1F` - Buttons, links, and key visual emphasis
- **Secondary Accent / Deep Gold**: `#544823` - Highlights, badges, charts, and supporting details

## Typography

- **Headers**: DM Sans Bold
- **Body Text**: Source Sans 3

## Best Used For

Food and beverage brands, hospitality decks, lifestyle campaigns, restaurant websites, seasonal promotions.
