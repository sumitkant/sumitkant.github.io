# Mountain Mist

A airy and tranquil theme built from cyan and teal tones that conveys ease, focus, and gentle momentum.

## Color Palette

- **Background / Light Cyan**: `#B0C4DE` - Chosen page and canvas background
- **Body Text / Charcoal**: `#5A5A5A` - Primary readable text color
- **Primary Accent / Deep Teal**: `#01796F` - Buttons, links, and key visual emphasis
- **Secondary Accent / Muted Cyan**: `#6D8196` - Highlights, badges, charts, and supporting details

## Typography

- **Headers**: Lexend Bold
- **Body Text**: Inter

## Best Used For

Wellness apps, travel guides, healthcare content, productivity tools, clean editorial experiences.
