# Salt and Pepper

A balanced and refined theme built from light gray and gray tones that conveys restraint, trust, and quiet sophistication.

## Color Palette

- **Background / White**: `#FFFFFF` - Chosen page and canvas background
- **Body Text / Black**: `#2B2B2B` - Primary readable text color
- **Primary Accent / Gray**: `#B3B3B3` - Buttons, links, and key visual emphasis
- **Secondary Accent / Light Gray**: `#D4D4D4` - Highlights, badges, charts, and supporting details

## Typography

- **Headers**: Inter Display Bold
- **Body Text**: Public Sans

## Best Used For

Corporate presentations, editorial layouts, premium retail, consulting reports, minimalist product interfaces.
