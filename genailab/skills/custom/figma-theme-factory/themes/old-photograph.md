# Old Photograph

A balanced and refined theme built from gold tones that conveys restraint, trust, and quiet sophistication.

## Color Palette

- **Background / Pale Gold**: `#FDFBD4` - Chosen page and canvas background
- **Body Text / Muted Gold**: `#545333` - Primary readable text color
- **Primary Accent / Light Gold**: `#D9D7B6` - Buttons, links, and key visual emphasis
- **Secondary Accent / Muted Gold**: `#878672` - Highlights, badges, charts, and supporting details

## Typography

- **Headers**: Plus Jakarta Sans Bold
- **Body Text**: Inter

## Best Used For

Corporate presentations, editorial layouts, premium retail, consulting reports, minimalist product interfaces.
