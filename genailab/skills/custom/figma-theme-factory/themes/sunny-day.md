# Sunny Day

A bright and energetic theme built from orange, gold, and cyan tones that conveys optimism, motion, and approachability.

## Color Palette

- **Background / Bright Orange**: `#FFBF00` - Chosen page and canvas background
- **Body Text / Bright Indigo**: `#2400FF` - Primary readable text color
- **Primary Accent / Bright Cyan**: `#007EFF` - Buttons, links, and key visual emphasis
- **Secondary Accent / Dark Gold**: `#807040` - Highlights, badges, charts, and supporting details

## Typography

- **Headers**: Poppins Bold
- **Body Text**: Figtree

## Best Used For

Consumer apps, children's products, edtech, travel promotions, social campaigns, lively dashboards.
