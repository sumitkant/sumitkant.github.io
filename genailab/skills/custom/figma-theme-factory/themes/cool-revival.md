# Cool Revival

A bold and high-impact theme built from teal, cyan, and mint tones that conveys experimentation, momentum, and attention.

## Color Palette

- **Background / Bright Teal**: `#00FFFF` - Chosen page and canvas background
- **Body Text / Bright Cyan**: `#00AEFF` - Primary readable text color
- **Primary Accent / Bright Mint**: `#00DE94` - Buttons, links, and key visual emphasis
- **Secondary Accent / Bright Green**: `#00FF52` - Highlights, badges, charts, and supporting details

## Typography

- **Headers**: Space Grotesk Bold
- **Body Text**: Inter

## Best Used For

AI products, gaming interfaces, music apps, creative campaigns, high-energy landing pages.
