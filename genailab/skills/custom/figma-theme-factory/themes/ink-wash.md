# Ink Wash

A polished and reliable theme built from charcoal, light gray, and gold tones that conveys clarity, confidence, and professional calm.

## Color Palette

- **Background / Pale Gold**: `#FFFFE3` - Chosen page and canvas background
- **Body Text / Charcoal**: `#4A4A4A` - Primary readable text color
- **Primary Accent / Muted Cyan**: `#6D8196` - Buttons, links, and key visual emphasis
- **Secondary Accent / Light Gray**: `#CBCBCB` - Highlights, badges, charts, and supporting details

## Typography

- **Headers**: Plus Jakarta Sans Bold
- **Body Text**: Inter

## Best Used For

SaaS dashboards, professional reports, knowledge products, fintech tools, enterprise websites.
