# Freshly Squeezed

A bright and energetic theme built from orange and gold tones that conveys optimism, motion, and approachability.

## Color Palette

- **Background / Bright Gold**: `#FFE642` - Chosen page and canvas background
- **Body Text / Bright Orange**: `#FF7900` - Primary readable text color
- **Primary Accent / Bright Orange**: `#FFBF00` - Buttons, links, and key visual emphasis
- **Secondary Accent / Light Orange**: `#F2CF7E` - Highlights, badges, charts, and supporting details

## Typography

- **Headers**: Poppins Bold
- **Body Text**: Figtree

## Best Used For

Food and beverage brands, hospitality decks, lifestyle campaigns, restaurant websites, seasonal promotions.
