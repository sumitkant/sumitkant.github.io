# Chili Spice

A polished and reliable theme built from red tones that conveys clarity, confidence, and professional calm.

## Color Palette

- **Background / Deep Burgundy**: `#38000A` - Chosen page and canvas background
- **Body Text / Light Red**: `#FFA896` - Primary readable text color
- **Primary Accent / Bright Red**: `#CD1C18` - Buttons, links, and key visual emphasis
- **Secondary Accent / Dark Red**: `#9B1313` - Highlights, badges, charts, and supporting details

## Typography

- **Headers**: Plus Jakarta Sans Bold
- **Body Text**: Inter

## Best Used For

Food and beverage brands, hospitality decks, lifestyle campaigns, restaurant websites, seasonal promotions.
