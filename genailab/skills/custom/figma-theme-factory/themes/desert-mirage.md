# Desert Mirage

A balanced and refined theme built from gold, red, and yellow tones that conveys restraint, trust, and quiet sophistication.

## Color Palette

- **Background / Soft Gold**: `#C2B280` - Chosen page and canvas background
- **Body Text / Deep Blue**: `#272757` - Primary readable text color
- **Primary Accent / Bright Red**: `#E35336` - Buttons, links, and key visual emphasis
- **Secondary Accent / Soft Yellow**: `#98A869` - Highlights, badges, charts, and supporting details

## Typography

- **Headers**: Inter Display Bold
- **Body Text**: Public Sans

## Best Used For

Corporate presentations, editorial layouts, premium retail, consulting reports, minimalist product interfaces.
