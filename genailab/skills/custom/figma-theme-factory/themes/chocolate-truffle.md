# Chocolate Truffle

A polished and reliable theme built from orange, orange-red, and gold tones that conveys clarity, confidence, and professional calm.

## Color Palette

- **Background / Deep Orange**: `#38240D` - Chosen page and canvas background
- **Body Text / Pale Gold**: `#FDFBD4` - Primary readable text color
- **Primary Accent / Dark Coral**: `#C05800` - Buttons, links, and key visual emphasis
- **Secondary Accent / Deep Orange**: `#713600` - Highlights, badges, charts, and supporting details

## Typography

- **Headers**: Plus Jakarta Sans Bold
- **Body Text**: Inter

## Best Used For

Food and beverage brands, hospitality decks, lifestyle campaigns, restaurant websites, seasonal promotions.
