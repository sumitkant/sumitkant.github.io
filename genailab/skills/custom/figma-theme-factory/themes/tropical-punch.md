# Tropical Punch

A bold and high-impact theme built from orange-red, pink, and gold tones that conveys experimentation, momentum, and attention.

## Color Palette

- **Background / Light Gold**: `#FCE883` - Chosen page and canvas background
- **Body Text / Dark Teal**: `#069494` - Primary readable text color
- **Primary Accent / Bright Coral**: `#FF8243` - Buttons, links, and key visual emphasis
- **Secondary Accent / Pale Pink**: `#FFC0CB` - Highlights, badges, charts, and supporting details

## Typography

- **Headers**: Space Grotesk Bold
- **Body Text**: Inter

## Best Used For

Food and beverage brands, hospitality decks, lifestyle campaigns, restaurant websites, seasonal promotions.
