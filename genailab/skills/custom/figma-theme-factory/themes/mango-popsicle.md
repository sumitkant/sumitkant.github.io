# Mango Popsicle

A bright and energetic theme built from orange and gold tones that conveys optimism, motion, and approachability.

## Color Palette

- **Background / Bright Gold**: `#F2E829` - Chosen page and canvas background
- **Body Text / Bright Coral**: `#F27430` - Primary readable text color
- **Primary Accent / Bright Orange**: `#F2B949` - Buttons, links, and key visual emphasis
- **Secondary Accent / Bright Gold**: `#EDD377` - Highlights, badges, charts, and supporting details

## Typography

- **Headers**: Poppins Bold
- **Body Text**: Figtree

## Best Used For

Food and beverage brands, hospitality decks, lifestyle campaigns, restaurant websites, seasonal promotions.
