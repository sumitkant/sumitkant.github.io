# Electropop

A bold and high-impact theme built from yellow, orange-red, and violet tones that conveys experimentation, momentum, and attention.

## Color Palette

- **Background / Bright Indigo**: `#5200FF` - Chosen page and canvas background
- **Body Text / Bright Yellow**: `#CCFF00` - Primary readable text color
- **Primary Accent / Bright Coral**: `#FF6B00` - Buttons, links, and key visual emphasis
- **Secondary Accent / Bright Violet**: `#F900FF` - Highlights, badges, charts, and supporting details

## Typography

- **Headers**: Space Grotesk Bold
- **Body Text**: Inter

## Best Used For

AI products, gaming interfaces, music apps, creative campaigns, high-energy landing pages.
