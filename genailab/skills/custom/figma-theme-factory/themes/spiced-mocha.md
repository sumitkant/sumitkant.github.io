# Spiced Mocha

A balanced and refined theme built from orange-red, orange, and gold tones that conveys restraint, trust, and quiet sophistication.

## Color Palette

- **Background / Deep Orange**: `#6D3B07` - Chosen page and canvas background
- **Body Text / Pale Gold**: `#F5F5DC` - Primary readable text color
- **Primary Accent / Soft Orange**: `#D47E30` - Buttons, links, and key visual emphasis
- **Secondary Accent / Dark Coral**: `#6F4E37` - Highlights, badges, charts, and supporting details

## Typography

- **Headers**: Plus Jakarta Sans Bold
- **Body Text**: Inter

## Best Used For

Food and beverage brands, hospitality decks, lifestyle campaigns, restaurant websites, seasonal promotions.
