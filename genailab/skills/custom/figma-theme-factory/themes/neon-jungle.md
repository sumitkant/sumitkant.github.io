# Neon Jungle

A bold and high-impact theme built from lime and pink tones that conveys experimentation, momentum, and attention.

## Color Palette

- **Background / Bright Violet**: `#A835F2` - Chosen page and canvas background
- **Body Text / Bright Lime**: `#89F336` - Primary readable text color
- **Primary Accent / Bright Pink**: `#F23598` - Buttons, links, and key visual emphasis
- **Secondary Accent / Dark Lime**: `#38A711` - Highlights, badges, charts, and supporting details

## Typography

- **Headers**: Space Grotesk Bold
- **Body Text**: Inter

## Best Used For

AI products, gaming interfaces, music apps, creative campaigns, high-energy landing pages.
