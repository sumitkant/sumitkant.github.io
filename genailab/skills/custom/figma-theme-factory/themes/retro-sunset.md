# Retro Sunset

A bright and energetic theme built from orange-red, gold, and teal tones that conveys optimism, motion, and approachability.

## Color Palette

- **Background / Bright Gold**: `#FFCE1B` - Chosen page and canvas background
- **Body Text / Dark Coral**: `#B7410E` - Primary readable text color
- **Primary Accent / Dark Coral**: `#BE5103` - Buttons, links, and key visual emphasis
- **Secondary Accent / Dark Teal**: `#069494` - Highlights, badges, charts, and supporting details

## Typography

- **Headers**: Poppins Bold
- **Body Text**: Figtree

## Best Used For

Consumer apps, children's products, edtech, travel promotions, social campaigns, lively dashboards.
