# Night Sands

A balanced and refined theme built from gold and orange tones that conveys restraint, trust, and quiet sophistication.

## Color Palette

- **Background / Deep Orange**: `#574A24` - Chosen page and canvas background
- **Body Text / Light Orange**: `#FAE8B4` - Primary readable text color
- **Primary Accent / Soft Gold**: `#CBBD93` - Buttons, links, and key visual emphasis
- **Secondary Accent / Muted Gold**: `#80775C` - Highlights, badges, charts, and supporting details

## Typography

- **Headers**: Plus Jakarta Sans Bold
- **Body Text**: Inter

## Best Used For

Corporate presentations, editorial layouts, premium retail, consulting reports, minimalist product interfaces.
