# Cozy Campfire

A balanced and refined theme built from orange-red and orange tones that conveys restraint, trust, and quiet sophistication.

## Color Palette

- **Background / Deep Red**: `#660F09` - Chosen page and canvas background
- **Body Text / Soft Orange**: `#AD7A32` - Primary readable text color
- **Primary Accent / Dark Orange**: `#8A6E29` - Buttons, links, and key visual emphasis
- **Secondary Accent / Dark Coral**: `#895129` - Highlights, badges, charts, and supporting details

## Typography

- **Headers**: Plus Jakarta Sans Bold
- **Body Text**: Inter

## Best Used For

Corporate presentations, editorial layouts, premium retail, consulting reports, minimalist product interfaces.
