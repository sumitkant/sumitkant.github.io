# Yacht Club

A balanced and refined theme built from orange-red, gray, and cyan tones that conveys restraint, trust, and quiet sophistication.

## Color Palette

- **Background / Muted Coral**: `#F2F0EF` - Chosen page and canvas background
- **Body Text / Dark Coral**: `#733E24` - Primary readable text color
- **Primary Accent / Dark Cyan**: `#245F73` - Buttons, links, and key visual emphasis
- **Secondary Accent / Gray**: `#BBBDBC` - Highlights, badges, charts, and supporting details

## Typography

- **Headers**: Inter Display Bold
- **Body Text**: Public Sans

## Best Used For

Corporate presentations, editorial layouts, premium retail, consulting reports, minimalist product interfaces.
