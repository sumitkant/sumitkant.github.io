# Glowing Horizon

A bright and energetic theme built from orange, teal, and blue tones that conveys optimism, motion, and approachability.

## Color Palette

- **Background / Bright Teal**: `#42EAFF` - Chosen page and canvas background
- **Body Text / Bright Blue**: `#4272FF` - Primary readable text color
- **Primary Accent / Bright Coral**: `#FF7E42` - Buttons, links, and key visual emphasis
- **Secondary Accent / Bright Orange**: `#FFB343` - Highlights, badges, charts, and supporting details

## Typography

- **Headers**: Poppins Bold
- **Body Text**: Figtree

## Best Used For

Consumer apps, children's products, edtech, travel promotions, social campaigns, lively dashboards.
