# Urban Graffiti

A bold and high-impact theme built from red, green, and teal tones that conveys experimentation, momentum, and attention.

## Color Palette

- **Background / Charcoal**: `#5A5A5A` - Chosen page and canvas background
- **Body Text / Bright Green**: `#2CFF05` - Primary readable text color
- **Primary Accent / Bright Teal**: `#00F0FF` - Buttons, links, and key visual emphasis
- **Secondary Accent / Bright Red**: `#FF2400` - Highlights, badges, charts, and supporting details

## Typography

- **Headers**: Space Grotesk Bold
- **Body Text**: Inter

## Best Used For

AI products, gaming interfaces, music apps, creative campaigns, high-energy landing pages.
