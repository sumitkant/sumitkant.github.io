# Urban Loft

A balanced and refined theme built from navy, orange-red, and charcoal tones that conveys restraint, trust, and quiet sophistication.

## Color Palette

- **Background / Black**: `#000000` - Chosen page and canvas background
- **Body Text / Soft Coral**: `#A35E47` - Primary readable text color
- **Primary Accent / Deep Navy**: `#0C0A1A` - Buttons, links, and key visual emphasis
- **Secondary Accent / Charcoal**: `#464646` - Highlights, badges, charts, and supporting details

## Typography

- **Headers**: Inter Display Bold
- **Body Text**: Public Sans

## Best Used For

Corporate presentations, editorial layouts, premium retail, consulting reports, minimalist product interfaces.
