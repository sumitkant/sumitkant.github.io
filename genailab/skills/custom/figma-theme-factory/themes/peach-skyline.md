# Peach Skyline

A soft and expressive theme built from orange, cyan, and mint tones that conveys warmth, elegance, and invitation.

## Color Palette

- **Background / Pale Mint**: `#BAFFF5` - Chosen page and canvas background
- **Body Text / Dark Cyan**: `#496580` - Primary readable text color
- **Primary Accent / Pale Cyan**: `#BADDFF` - Buttons, links, and key visual emphasis
- **Secondary Accent / Pale Orange**: `#FFDBBB` - Highlights, badges, charts, and supporting details

## Typography

- **Headers**: DM Sans Bold
- **Body Text**: Source Sans 3

## Best Used For

Food and beverage brands, hospitality decks, lifestyle campaigns, restaurant websites, seasonal promotions.
