# Dusk Horizon

A color system that feels warm, cinematic, and optimistic, balancing strong brand accents with usable neutrals for polished digital interfaces and presentation design.

## Color Palette

- **Deep Sky Blue**: `#083D77` - Foundation color for headers, navigation, and high-contrast surfaces
- **Pale Gold**: `#EBEBD3` - Soft backgrounds, highlight panels, and secondary content blocks
- **Warm Amber**: `#F4D35E` - Warm calls-to-action, badges, pricing highlights, and emphasis states
- **Warm Orange**: `#EE964B` - Supporting accent for illustrations, dividers, icon fills, and content hierarchy
- **Warm Red**: `#F95738` - Alert accents, energetic CTAs, campaign details, and emotional highlights

## Typography

- **Headers**: Plus Jakarta Sans
- **Body Text**: Inter
- **Font Direction**: Plus Jakarta Sans offers a premium, modern, and approachable headline voice, while Inter keeps the body system highly readable.

## Best Used For

Lifestyle campaigns, media kits, event branding, restaurant concepts, travel stories, and warm marketing presentations.
