# Warm Autumn Glow

A color system that feels polished, premium, and refined, balancing strong brand accents with usable neutrals for polished digital interfaces and presentation design.

## Color Palette

- **Midnight Cyan**: `#003049` - Foundation color for headers, navigation, and high-contrast surfaces
- **Vivid Red**: `#D62828` - Alert accents, energetic CTAs, campaign details, and emotional highlights
- **Vivid Orange**: `#F77F00` - Supporting accent for illustrations, dividers, icon fills, and content hierarchy
- **Warm Amber**: `#FCBF49` - Warm calls-to-action, badges, pricing highlights, and emphasis states
- **Pale Gold**: `#EAE2B7` - Soft backgrounds, highlight panels, and secondary content blocks

## Typography

- **Headers**: Plus Jakarta Sans
- **Body Text**: Inter
- **Font Direction**: Plus Jakarta Sans reinforces the premium, polished palette personality, while Inter keeps long-form product copy neutral and readable.

## Best Used For

Premium SaaS, fintech, luxury packaging, executive presentations, portfolio sites, and polished product launches.
