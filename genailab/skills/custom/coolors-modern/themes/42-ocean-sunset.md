# Ocean Sunset

A color system that feels cool, coastal, and trustworthy, balancing strong brand accents with usable neutrals for polished digital interfaces and presentation design.

## Color Palette

- **Balanced Sky Blue**: `#586BA4` - Foundation color for headers, navigation, and high-contrast surfaces
- **Muted Sky Blue**: `#324376` - Primary interface accents, links, charts, and trust-building UI elements
- **Pale Amber**: `#F5DD90` - Soft backgrounds, highlight panels, and secondary content blocks
- **Warm Coral**: `#F68E5F` - Supporting accent for illustrations, dividers, icon fills, and content hierarchy
- **Warm Red**: `#F76C5E` - Alert accents, energetic CTAs, campaign details, and emotional highlights

## Typography

- **Headers**: Plus Jakarta Sans
- **Body Text**: Inter
- **Font Direction**: Plus Jakarta Sans adds premium modernity to the cool aquatic palette, while Inter provides a trustworthy product-reading experience.

## Best Used For

Travel products, wellness apps, hospitality branding, clean SaaS dashboards, and coastal lifestyle campaigns.
