# Cozy Cabin

A color system that feels warm, grounded, and organic, balancing strong brand accents with usable neutrals for polished digital interfaces and presentation design.

## Color Palette

- **Warm Orange**: `#C3A995` - Supporting accent for illustrations, dividers, icon fills, and content hierarchy
- **Muted Earth**: `#AB947E` - Supporting accent for illustrations, dividers, icon fills, and content hierarchy
- **Muted Earth 2**: `#6F5E53` - Deep contrast layer for footers, app shells, and premium dark sections
- **Muted Earth 3**: `#8A7968` - Supporting accent for illustrations, dividers, icon fills, and content hierarchy
- **Muted Earth 4**: `#593D3B` - Alert accents, energetic CTAs, campaign details, and emotional highlights

## Typography

- **Headers**: DM Sans
- **Body Text**: Source Sans 3
- **Font Direction**: DM Sans brings warmth and humanity to the earthy palette, while Source Sans 3 supports mature, editorial body content.

## Best Used For

Sustainability brands, outdoor products, food and beverage, hospitality, seasonal campaigns, and wellness content.
