# Monochrome Harmony

A color system that feels structured, mature, and product-focused, balancing strong brand accents with usable neutrals for polished digital interfaces and presentation design.

## Color Palette

- **Pale Mint**: `#CFDBD5` - Soft backgrounds, highlight panels, and secondary content blocks
- **Soft Olive**: `#E8EDDF` - Soft backgrounds, highlight panels, and secondary content blocks
- **Warm Amber**: `#F5CB5C` - Warm calls-to-action, badges, pricing highlights, and emphasis states
- **Pure Black**: `#242423` - Deep contrast layer for footers, app shells, and premium dark sections
- **Charcoal**: `#333533` - Success states, nature cues, wellness accents, and calm supporting elements

## Typography

- **Headers**: Inter Display
- **Body Text**: Public Sans
- **Font Direction**: Inter Display and Public Sans support a serious enterprise feel with authoritative, dependable readability.

## Best Used For

Enterprise dashboards, design systems, compliance software, architecture portfolios, data products, and documentation.
