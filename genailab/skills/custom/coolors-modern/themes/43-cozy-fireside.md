# Cozy Fireside

A color system that feels warm, grounded, and organic, balancing strong brand accents with usable neutrals for polished digital interfaces and presentation design.

## Color Palette

- **Midnight Rose**: `#231F20` - Foundation color for headers, navigation, and high-contrast surfaces
- **Balanced Red**: `#BB4430` - Alert accents, energetic CTAs, campaign details, and emotional highlights
- **Cool Teal**: `#7EBDC2` - Primary interface accents, links, charts, and trust-building UI elements
- **Pale Amber**: `#F3DFA2` - Soft backgrounds, highlight panels, and secondary content blocks
- **Pale Orange**: `#EFE6DD` - Soft backgrounds, highlight panels, and secondary content blocks

## Typography

- **Headers**: DM Sans
- **Body Text**: Source Sans 3
- **Font Direction**: DM Sans brings warmth and humanity to the earthy palette, while Source Sans 3 supports mature, editorial body content.

## Best Used For

Sustainability brands, outdoor products, food and beverage, hospitality, seasonal campaigns, and wellness content.
