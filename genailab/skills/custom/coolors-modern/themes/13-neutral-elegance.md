# Neutral Elegance

A color system that feels polished, premium, and refined, balancing strong brand accents with usable neutrals for polished digital interfaces and presentation design.

## Color Palette

- **Muted Earth**: `#463F3A` - Foundation color for headers, navigation, and high-contrast surfaces
- **Muted Earth 2**: `#8A817C` - Supporting accent for illustrations, dividers, icon fills, and content hierarchy
- **Warm Amber**: `#BCB8B1` - Supporting accent for illustrations, dividers, icon fills, and content hierarchy
- **Soft White**: `#F4F3EE` - Clean page backgrounds, cards, spacious UI areas, and reversed text zones
- **Warm Coral**: `#E0AFA0` - Alert accents, energetic CTAs, campaign details, and emotional highlights

## Typography

- **Headers**: Plus Jakarta Sans
- **Body Text**: Inter
- **Font Direction**: Plus Jakarta Sans reinforces the premium, polished palette personality, while Inter keeps long-form product copy neutral and readable.

## Best Used For

Premium SaaS, fintech, luxury packaging, executive presentations, portfolio sites, and polished product launches.
