# Mystic Waters

A color system that feels cool, coastal, and trustworthy, balancing strong brand accents with usable neutrals for polished digital interfaces and presentation design.

## Color Palette

- **Midnight Cyan**: `#031926` - Foundation color for headers, navigation, and high-contrast surfaces
- **Balanced Teal**: `#468189` - Primary interface accents, links, charts, and trust-building UI elements
- **Balanced Mint**: `#77ACA2` - Supporting accent for illustrations, dividers, icon fills, and content hierarchy
- **Cool Teal**: `#9DBEBB` - Supporting accent for illustrations, dividers, icon fills, and content hierarchy
- **Ivory**: `#F4E9CD` - Soft backgrounds, highlight panels, and secondary content blocks

## Typography

- **Headers**: Plus Jakarta Sans
- **Body Text**: Inter
- **Font Direction**: Plus Jakarta Sans adds premium modernity to the cool aquatic palette, while Inter provides a trustworthy product-reading experience.

## Best Used For

Travel products, wellness apps, hospitality branding, clean SaaS dashboards, and coastal lifestyle campaigns.
