# Forest Adventure

A color system that feels warm, grounded, and organic, balancing strong brand accents with usable neutrals for polished digital interfaces and presentation design.

## Color Palette

- **Pure Black**: `#131515` - Foundation color for headers, navigation, and high-contrast surfaces
- **Pure Black 2**: `#2B2C28` - Deep contrast layer for footers, app shells, and premium dark sections
- **Balanced Teal**: `#339989` - Supporting accent for illustrations, dividers, icon fills, and content hierarchy
- **Soft Mint**: `#7DE2D1` - Supporting accent for illustrations, dividers, icon fills, and content hierarchy
- **Pale Rose**: `#FFFAFB` - Clean page backgrounds, cards, spacious UI areas, and reversed text zones

## Typography

- **Headers**: Outfit
- **Body Text**: Figtree
- **Font Direction**: Outfit gives the organic palette a contemporary, design-forward voice, while Figtree keeps the experience friendly and consumer-ready.

## Best Used For

Sustainability brands, outdoor products, food and beverage, hospitality, seasonal campaigns, and wellness content.
