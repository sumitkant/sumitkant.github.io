# Dark Sunset

A color system that feels warm, cinematic, and optimistic, balancing strong brand accents with usable neutrals for polished digital interfaces and presentation design.

## Color Palette

- **Muted Cyan**: `#335C67` - Foundation color for headers, navigation, and high-contrast surfaces
- **Pale Gold**: `#FFF3B0` - Clean page backgrounds, cards, spacious UI areas, and reversed text zones
- **Vivid Orange**: `#E09F3E` - Warm calls-to-action, badges, pricing highlights, and emphasis states
- **Rich Rose**: `#9E2A2B` - Alert accents, energetic CTAs, campaign details, and emotional highlights
- **Deep Rose**: `#540B0E` - Alert accents, energetic CTAs, campaign details, and emotional highlights

## Typography

- **Headers**: Plus Jakarta Sans
- **Body Text**: Inter
- **Font Direction**: Plus Jakarta Sans offers a premium, modern, and approachable headline voice, while Inter keeps the body system highly readable.

## Best Used For

Lifestyle campaigns, media kits, event branding, restaurant concepts, travel stories, and warm marketing presentations.
