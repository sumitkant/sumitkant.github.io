# Sky Blue Freshness

A color system that feels modern, balanced, and versatile, balancing strong brand accents with usable neutrals for polished digital interfaces and presentation design.

## Color Palette

- **Rich Cyan**: `#05668D` - Foundation color for headers, navigation, and high-contrast surfaces
- **Balanced Cyan**: `#427AA1` - Primary interface accents, links, charts, and trust-building UI elements
- **Pale Sky Blue**: `#EBF2FA` - Clean page backgrounds, cards, spacious UI areas, and reversed text zones
- **Rich Green**: `#679436` - Supporting accent for illustrations, dividers, icon fills, and content hierarchy
- **Rich Lime**: `#A5BE00` - Supporting accent for illustrations, dividers, icon fills, and content hierarchy

## Typography

- **Headers**: Lexend
- **Body Text**: Inter
- **Font Direction**: Lexend adds an accessible, human-centered quality to the fresh palette, while Inter keeps body text crisp and professional.

## Best Used For

Modern SaaS products, brand systems, campaign pages, presentation decks, dashboards, and product marketing assets.
