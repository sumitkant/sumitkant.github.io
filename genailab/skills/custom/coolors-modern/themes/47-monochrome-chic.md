# Monochrome Chic

A color system that feels polished, premium, and refined, balancing strong brand accents with usable neutrals for polished digital interfaces and presentation design.

## Color Palette

- **Charcoal**: `#474448` - Foundation color for headers, navigation, and high-contrast surfaces
- **Midnight Purple**: `#2D232E` - Creative accents, hero graphics, product moments, and expressive highlights
- **Pale Amber**: `#E0DDCF` - Soft backgrounds, highlight panels, and secondary content blocks
- **Charcoal 2**: `#534B52` - Creative accents, hero graphics, product moments, and expressive highlights
- **Soft White**: `#F1F0EA` - Clean page backgrounds, cards, spacious UI areas, and reversed text zones

## Typography

- **Headers**: Inter Display
- **Body Text**: Public Sans
- **Font Direction**: Inter Display and Public Sans support a serious enterprise feel with authoritative, dependable readability.

## Best Used For

Premium SaaS, fintech, luxury packaging, executive presentations, portfolio sites, and polished product launches.
