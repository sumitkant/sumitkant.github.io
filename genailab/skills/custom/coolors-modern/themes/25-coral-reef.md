# Coral Reef

A color system that feels modern, balanced, and versatile, balancing strong brand accents with usable neutrals for polished digital interfaces and presentation design.

## Color Palette

- **Balanced Teal**: `#588B8B` - Primary interface accents, links, charts, and trust-building UI elements
- **Crisp White**: `#FFFFFF` - Clean page backgrounds, cards, spacious UI areas, and reversed text zones
- **Pale Coral**: `#FFD5C2` - Soft backgrounds, highlight panels, and secondary content blocks
- **Warm Orange**: `#F28F3B` - Supporting accent for illustrations, dividers, icon fills, and content hierarchy
- **Balanced Red**: `#C8553D` - Alert accents, energetic CTAs, campaign details, and emotional highlights

## Typography

- **Headers**: Outfit
- **Body Text**: Figtree
- **Font Direction**: Outfit gives the organic palette a contemporary, design-forward voice, while Figtree keeps the experience friendly and consumer-ready.

## Best Used For

Modern SaaS products, brand systems, campaign pages, presentation decks, dashboards, and product marketing assets.
