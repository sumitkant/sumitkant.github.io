# Neutral Earth Tones

A color system that feels warm, grounded, and organic, balancing strong brand accents with usable neutrals for polished digital interfaces and presentation design.

## Color Palette

- **Muted Earth**: `#0A0908` - Foundation color for headers, navigation, and high-contrast surfaces
- **Deep Cyan**: `#22333B` - Primary interface accents, links, charts, and trust-building UI elements
- **Pale Orange**: `#EAE0D5` - Soft backgrounds, highlight panels, and secondary content blocks
- **Warm Orange**: `#C6AC8F` - Supporting accent for illustrations, dividers, icon fills, and content hierarchy
- **Muted Earth 2**: `#5E503F` - Deep contrast layer for footers, app shells, and premium dark sections

## Typography

- **Headers**: DM Sans
- **Body Text**: Source Sans 3
- **Font Direction**: DM Sans brings warmth and humanity to the earthy palette, while Source Sans 3 supports mature, editorial body content.

## Best Used For

Sustainability brands, outdoor products, food and beverage, hospitality, seasonal campaigns, and wellness content.
