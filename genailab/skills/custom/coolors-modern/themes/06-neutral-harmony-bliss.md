# Neutral Harmony Bliss

A color system that feels structured, mature, and product-focused, balancing strong brand accents with usable neutrals for polished digital interfaces and presentation design.

## Color Palette

- **Ivory**: `#F4F1DE` - Clean page backgrounds, cards, spacious UI areas, and reversed text zones
- **Warm Coral**: `#E07A5F` - Alert accents, energetic CTAs, campaign details, and emotional highlights
- **Muted Blue**: `#3D405B` - Primary interface accents, links, charts, and trust-building UI elements
- **Soft Mint**: `#81B29A` - Success states, nature cues, wellness accents, and calm supporting elements
- **Warm Orange**: `#F2CC8F` - Warm calls-to-action, badges, pricing highlights, and emphasis states

## Typography

- **Headers**: Plus Jakarta Sans
- **Body Text**: Inter
- **Font Direction**: Plus Jakarta Sans offers a premium, modern, and approachable headline voice, while Inter keeps the body system highly readable.

## Best Used For

Enterprise dashboards, design systems, compliance software, architecture portfolios, data products, and documentation.
