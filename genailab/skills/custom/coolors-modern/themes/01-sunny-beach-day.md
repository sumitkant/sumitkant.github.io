# Sunny Beach Day

A color system that feels cool, coastal, and trustworthy, balancing strong brand accents with usable neutrals for polished digital interfaces and presentation design.

## Color Palette

- **Deep Cyan**: `#264653` - Foundation color for headers, navigation, and high-contrast surfaces
- **Rich Teal**: `#2A9D8F` - Supporting accent for illustrations, dividers, icon fills, and content hierarchy
- **Warm Amber**: `#E9C46A` - Warm calls-to-action, badges, pricing highlights, and emphasis states
- **Warm Orange**: `#F4A261` - Supporting accent for illustrations, dividers, icon fills, and content hierarchy
- **Warm Coral**: `#E76F51` - Alert accents, energetic CTAs, campaign details, and emotional highlights

## Typography

- **Headers**: Plus Jakarta Sans
- **Body Text**: Inter
- **Font Direction**: Plus Jakarta Sans adds premium modernity to the cool aquatic palette, while Inter provides a trustworthy product-reading experience.

## Best Used For

Travel products, wellness apps, hospitality branding, clean SaaS dashboards, and coastal lifestyle campaigns.
