# Golden Glow

A color system that feels polished, premium, and refined, balancing strong brand accents with usable neutrals for polished digital interfaces and presentation design.

## Color Palette

- **Rich Gold**: `#7C6A0A` - Foundation color for headers, navigation, and high-contrast surfaces
- **Warm Gold**: `#BABD8D` - Supporting accent for illustrations, dividers, icon fills, and content hierarchy
- **Pale Coral**: `#FFDAC6` - Soft backgrounds, highlight panels, and secondary content blocks
- **Vivid Orange**: `#FA9500` - Warm calls-to-action, badges, pricing highlights, and emphasis states
- **Vivid Coral**: `#EB6424` - Supporting accent for illustrations, dividers, icon fills, and content hierarchy

## Typography

- **Headers**: Plus Jakarta Sans
- **Body Text**: Inter
- **Font Direction**: Plus Jakarta Sans reinforces the premium, polished palette personality, while Inter keeps long-form product copy neutral and readable.

## Best Used For

Premium SaaS, fintech, luxury packaging, executive presentations, portfolio sites, and polished product launches.
