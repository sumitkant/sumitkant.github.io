# Contrast Pop

A color system that feels expressive, creative, and high-energy, balancing strong brand accents with usable neutrals for polished digital interfaces and presentation design.

## Color Palette

- **Midnight Sky Blue**: `#011627` - Foundation color for headers, navigation, and high-contrast surfaces
- **Pale Green**: `#FDFFFC` - Clean page backgrounds, cards, spacious UI areas, and reversed text zones
- **Balanced Teal**: `#2EC4B6` - Supporting accent for illustrations, dividers, icon fills, and content hierarchy
- **Vivid Rose**: `#E71D36` - Alert accents, energetic CTAs, campaign details, and emotional highlights
- **Vivid Orange**: `#FF9F1C` - Supporting accent for illustrations, dividers, icon fills, and content hierarchy

## Typography

- **Headers**: Poppins
- **Body Text**: Figtree
- **Font Direction**: Poppins matches the energetic, marketing-forward color contrast, while Figtree keeps body copy casual, modern, and approachable.

## Best Used For

GenAI tools, creative platforms, launch pages, entertainment brands, social campaigns, and expressive dashboards.
