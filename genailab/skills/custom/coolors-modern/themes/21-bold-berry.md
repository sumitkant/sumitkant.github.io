# Bold Berry

A color system that feels expressive, creative, and high-energy, balancing strong brand accents with usable neutrals for polished digital interfaces and presentation design.

## Color Palette

- **Pale Orange**: `#F9DBBD` - Soft backgrounds, highlight panels, and secondary content blocks
- **Pale Rose**: `#FFA5AB` - Alert accents, energetic CTAs, campaign details, and emotional highlights
- **Warm Rose**: `#DA627D` - Alert accents, energetic CTAs, campaign details, and emotional highlights
- **Balanced Magenta**: `#A53860` - Deep contrast layer for footers, app shells, and premium dark sections
- **Midnight Magenta**: `#450920` - Deep contrast layer for footers, app shells, and premium dark sections

## Typography

- **Headers**: Plus Jakarta Sans
- **Body Text**: Inter
- **Font Direction**: Plus Jakarta Sans offers a premium, modern, and approachable headline voice, while Inter keeps the body system highly readable.

## Best Used For

GenAI tools, creative platforms, launch pages, entertainment brands, social campaigns, and expressive dashboards.
