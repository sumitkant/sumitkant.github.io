# Earthly Tones

A color system that feels warm, grounded, and organic, balancing strong brand accents with usable neutrals for polished digital interfaces and presentation design.

## Color Palette

- **Muted Blue**: `#555B6E` - Foundation color for headers, navigation, and high-contrast surfaces
- **Cool Teal**: `#89B0AE` - Supporting accent for illustrations, dividers, icon fills, and content hierarchy
- **Pale Mint**: `#BEE3DB` - Soft backgrounds, highlight panels, and secondary content blocks
- **Soft White**: `#FAF9F9` - Clean page backgrounds, cards, spacious UI areas, and reversed text zones
- **Pale Coral**: `#FFD6BA` - Soft backgrounds, highlight panels, and secondary content blocks

## Typography

- **Headers**: DM Sans
- **Body Text**: Source Sans 3
- **Font Direction**: DM Sans brings warmth and humanity to the earthy palette, while Source Sans 3 supports mature, editorial body content.

## Best Used For

Sustainability brands, outdoor products, food and beverage, hospitality, seasonal campaigns, and wellness content.
