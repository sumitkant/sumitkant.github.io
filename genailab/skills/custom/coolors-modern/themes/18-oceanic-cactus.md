# Oceanic Cactus

A color system that feels cool, coastal, and trustworthy, balancing strong brand accents with usable neutrals for polished digital interfaces and presentation design.

## Color Palette

- **Deep Teal**: `#1A535C` - Foundation color for headers, navigation, and high-contrast surfaces
- **Balanced Teal**: `#4ECDC4` - Supporting accent for illustrations, dividers, icon fills, and content hierarchy
- **Pale Green**: `#F7FFF7` - Clean page backgrounds, cards, spacious UI areas, and reversed text zones
- **Warm Red**: `#FF6B6B` - Alert accents, energetic CTAs, campaign details, and emotional highlights
- **Warm Amber**: `#FFE66D` - Soft backgrounds, highlight panels, and secondary content blocks

## Typography

- **Headers**: Plus Jakarta Sans
- **Body Text**: Inter
- **Font Direction**: Plus Jakarta Sans adds premium modernity to the cool aquatic palette, while Inter provides a trustworthy product-reading experience.

## Best Used For

Travel products, wellness apps, hospitality branding, clean SaaS dashboards, and coastal lifestyle campaigns.
