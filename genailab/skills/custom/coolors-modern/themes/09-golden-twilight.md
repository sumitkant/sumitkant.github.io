# Golden Twilight

A color system that feels polished, premium, and refined, balancing strong brand accents with usable neutrals for polished digital interfaces and presentation design.

## Color Palette

- **Midnight Sky Blue**: `#000814` - Foundation color for headers, navigation, and high-contrast surfaces
- **Midnight Sky Blue 2**: `#001D3D` - Primary interface accents, links, charts, and trust-building UI elements
- **Deep Sky Blue**: `#003566` - Primary interface accents, links, charts, and trust-building UI elements
- **Vivid Amber**: `#FFC300` - Warm calls-to-action, badges, pricing highlights, and emphasis states
- **Vivid Amber 2**: `#FFD60A` - Soft backgrounds, highlight panels, and secondary content blocks

## Typography

- **Headers**: Plus Jakarta Sans
- **Body Text**: Inter
- **Font Direction**: Plus Jakarta Sans reinforces the premium, polished palette personality, while Inter keeps long-form product copy neutral and readable.

## Best Used For

Premium SaaS, fintech, luxury packaging, executive presentations, portfolio sites, and polished product launches.
