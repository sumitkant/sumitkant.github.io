# Autumn Hues

A color system that feels warm, grounded, and organic, balancing strong brand accents with usable neutrals for polished digital interfaces and presentation design.

## Color Palette

- **Pale Red**: `#EEE2DF` - Soft backgrounds, highlight panels, and secondary content blocks
- **Pale Orange**: `#EED7C5` - Soft backgrounds, highlight panels, and secondary content blocks
- **Warm Red**: `#C89F9C` - Alert accents, energetic CTAs, campaign details, and emotional highlights
- **Balanced Coral**: `#C97C5D` - Alert accents, energetic CTAs, campaign details, and emotional highlights
- **Balanced Red**: `#B36A5E` - Alert accents, energetic CTAs, campaign details, and emotional highlights

## Typography

- **Headers**: DM Sans
- **Body Text**: Source Sans 3
- **Font Direction**: DM Sans brings warmth and humanity to the earthy palette, while Source Sans 3 supports mature, editorial body content.

## Best Used For

Sustainability brands, outdoor products, food and beverage, hospitality, seasonal campaigns, and wellness content.
