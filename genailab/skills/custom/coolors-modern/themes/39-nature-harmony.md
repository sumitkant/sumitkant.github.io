# Nature Harmony

A color system that feels warm, grounded, and organic, balancing strong brand accents with usable neutrals for polished digital interfaces and presentation design.

## Color Palette

- **Soft White**: `#EFF1ED` - Clean page backgrounds, cards, spacious UI areas, and reversed text zones
- **Muted Olive**: `#373D20` - Deep contrast layer for footers, app shells, and premium dark sections
- **Muted Olive 2**: `#717744` - Deep contrast layer for footers, app shells, and premium dark sections
- **Warm Gold**: `#BCBD8B` - Supporting accent for illustrations, dividers, icon fills, and content hierarchy
- **Muted Earth**: `#766153` - Deep contrast layer for footers, app shells, and premium dark sections

## Typography

- **Headers**: DM Sans
- **Body Text**: Source Sans 3
- **Font Direction**: DM Sans brings warmth and humanity to the earthy palette, while Source Sans 3 supports mature, editorial body content.

## Best Used For

Sustainability brands, outdoor products, food and beverage, hospitality, seasonal campaigns, and wellness content.
