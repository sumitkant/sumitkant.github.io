# Cool Coastal Vibes

A color system that feels cool, coastal, and trustworthy, balancing strong brand accents with usable neutrals for polished digital interfaces and presentation design.

## Color Palette

- **Deep Blue**: `#2B2D42` - Foundation color for headers, navigation, and high-contrast surfaces
- **Cool Sky Blue**: `#8D99AE` - Primary interface accents, links, charts, and trust-building UI elements
- **Soft White**: `#EDF2F4` - Clean page backgrounds, cards, spacious UI areas, and reversed text zones
- **Vivid Rose**: `#EF233C` - Alert accents, energetic CTAs, campaign details, and emotional highlights
- **Vivid Rose 2**: `#D90429` - Alert accents, energetic CTAs, campaign details, and emotional highlights

## Typography

- **Headers**: Plus Jakarta Sans
- **Body Text**: Inter
- **Font Direction**: Plus Jakarta Sans adds premium modernity to the cool aquatic palette, while Inter provides a trustworthy product-reading experience.

## Best Used For

Travel products, wellness apps, hospitality branding, clean SaaS dashboards, and coastal lifestyle campaigns.
