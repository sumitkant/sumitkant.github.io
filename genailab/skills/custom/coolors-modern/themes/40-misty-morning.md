# Misty Morning

A color system that feels soft, nostalgic, and editorial, balancing strong brand accents with usable neutrals for polished digital interfaces and presentation design.

## Color Palette

- **Deep Cyan**: `#223843` - Foundation color for headers, navigation, and high-contrast surfaces
- **Soft White**: `#EFF1F3` - Clean page backgrounds, cards, spacious UI areas, and reversed text zones
- **Pale Magenta**: `#DBD3D8` - Creative accents, hero graphics, product moments, and expressive highlights
- **Warm Coral**: `#D8B4A0` - Supporting accent for illustrations, dividers, icon fills, and content hierarchy
- **Warm Coral 2**: `#D77A61` - Alert accents, energetic CTAs, campaign details, and emotional highlights

## Typography

- **Headers**: DM Sans
- **Body Text**: Source Sans 3
- **Font Direction**: DM Sans brings warmth and humanity to the earthy palette, while Source Sans 3 supports mature, editorial body content.

## Best Used For

Editorial brands, boutique hospitality, storytelling decks, maker products, calm landing pages, and content-rich websites.
