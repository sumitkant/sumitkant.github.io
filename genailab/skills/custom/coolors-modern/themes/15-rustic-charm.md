# Rustic Charm

A color system that feels warm, grounded, and organic, balancing strong brand accents with usable neutrals for polished digital interfaces and presentation design.

## Color Palette

- **Ivory**: `#FFFCF2` - Clean page backgrounds, cards, spacious UI areas, and reversed text zones
- **Pale Orange**: `#CCC5B9` - Supporting accent for illustrations, dividers, icon fills, and content hierarchy
- **Muted Earth**: `#403D39` - Deep contrast layer for footers, app shells, and premium dark sections
- **Pure Black**: `#252422` - Deep contrast layer for footers, app shells, and premium dark sections
- **Vivid Coral**: `#EB5E28` - Alert accents, energetic CTAs, campaign details, and emotional highlights

## Typography

- **Headers**: DM Sans
- **Body Text**: Source Sans 3
- **Font Direction**: DM Sans brings warmth and humanity to the earthy palette, while Source Sans 3 supports mature, editorial body content.

## Best Used For

Sustainability brands, outdoor products, food and beverage, hospitality, seasonal campaigns, and wellness content.
